Why This Event Matters

Enterprise AI was meant to unlock growth, yet most organizations are stuck in pilot purgatory.

  • 74% of AI initiatives never scale beyond pilots.
  • 95% fail to deliver measurable ROI.

The problem is not algorithms. It is architecture. Customer data remains fragmented across CRM, CDP, loyalty systems, marketing tools, regions, and clouds.

Together with Microsoft, Socialhub.AI presents the Customer Intelligence Platform (CIP), an AI-native intelligence layer above CRM, CDP, and Marketing Automation to unify data, interpret intent, and orchestrate actions in real time.

Not another tool. A structural reset.
